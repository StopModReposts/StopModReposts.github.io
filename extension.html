StopModReposts Extension

We offer an extension to block reposting sites automatically.

You can use our browser extension for Chrome and Firefox if you'd like to guard yourself or your kids from accidentally downloading from malicious reposting sites. It's free and private.

 It's privacy-friendly (our extension doesn't collect any personal data)

 It's easy to use (runs automatically in the background and refreshes the lists on startup)

 It's simple (uncluttered and simple to use)
