Frequently Asked Questions

Some quick answers for frequently asked questions can be found here.

How can I get involved?

Please visit the "How to get involved" page for more information.

Do you have a Discord server?

Yes, you can join our Discord server here.

How can I add a site?

You can notify us about a site via the Submit Form on this page, the "Issues" tab on GitHub, or in our Discord server.

I reported something and didn't get a reply.

That's normal. We can't and wont reply to every report, it's still important to submit your email since we might have some questions.

What are you using the donations for?

We're using donated money to pay off domains and hosting (e.g. for our API). If we don't use all donations, the rest will be donated to EFF.

How can I use your API?

Check the GitHub Wiki here.

My site is listed even though I don't repost anything.

You can contact us via our Removal Form to get your site removed if it really doesn't violate our rules.
            
If you just purchased a listed domain: The domain owner before you might have done some illegal activities with your domain. Please contact us so we can remove your domain from our lists. Also contact your domain registrar if needed, to check, if your domain is off their abuse lists too. 

How do I comply with the rules as a site owner?

Contact us for a list of To-Do's. After you comply with the rules, we will remove your site from the list if it's listed.
